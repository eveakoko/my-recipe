Cocktail

Description

Irish whiskey, blended with citrus and hints of cherry, makes for a smooth
sipping cocktail. Garnish with additional lemon and orange if desired.

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon wedge
  • 1 orange slice
  • 1 fluid ounce irish whiskey
  • 1/2 fluid ounce amaretto liqueur
  • 1/2 fluid ounce triple sec
  • 1 dash bitters
  • 11/2 cups ice, divided

Steps

  1. Muddle lemon wedge and orange slice in a cocktail shaker. Add Irish whiskey,
    amaretto, triple sec, bitters, and 1 cup of ice. Close and shake 6 times.
  2. Fill a rocks glass with remaining 1/2 cup of ice and strain cocktail on top.
